Welcome to Our Hotel

N & S inn Hotel, a complete hospitality partner where guests can have a taste of multicultural cuisines with different kinds of facilities is serving since 05 years. Customer’s satisfaction is the main priority and it has aimed to be a top hotel and resort in Nepal. In addition, we have support for tour guidance and itinerary booking from our hotel from which guest can make their schedule by staying in our hotel room.

In the year between 2018-2019, almost around 10,000 guest had been stayed in N & S inn and got a very satisfactory compliment from most of them. We have planned to open a chain hotel restaurant all over the South Asia with aim to serve the varieties of dishes and culture.

Proper HR management, experienced Chefs and professional employees are the main property of our hotel and we sell not only foods but also shows professionalism in the hospitality food.
