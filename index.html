Welcome to Our Hotel

N & S Hotel is a global partnership hotel which is jointly running by the people of Nepal & Sri Lanka. The main aim of the hotel is to explore the varieties of cuisines from Nepal & Sri Lanka and provide quality service to our customers. It is located at Nagarkot Bhaktpur, 26 Km far from Kathmandu city and surrounded by beautiful hills and green forests. Bhaktapur is a rich cultural city which has many world heritage monuments.

The hotel represents traditional and cultural food items along with different handicraft materials which will helps to promote on the national and traditional cuisines of Nepal & Sri Lanka.

Meantime, the peaceful environment altogether with beautiful swimming pool, gym & spa, green garden, equipped bar and rooftop dinning facility makes guest chose N & S Hotel. Besides we have online booking and reservation system which is opened for 24 hours daily.
